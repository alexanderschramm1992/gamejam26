# FEATURES.md
This file describes features to be implemented by this game.

# Feature 1: Hud
The game is played by a single player in the canvas defined by #canvas. In the canvas a hud displayed at the top of the screen. The hud contains information like the player's score and playtime.

# Feature 2: Top down car driving
The player controls a car using arrow keys. The car speeds up and decelerates according to keys being held down. The car can also turn left and right. The car's speed is affected by the direction of the turn. The car can also drift when turning at high speeds. For the car use the sprite defined in /src/assets/images/car.png.

# Feature 2: Top down car shooting
The car should be shooting on mouse click on the spot where the mouse points.
